Our Story

A Unique Experience Built for Santa Barbara

The SB Trackless Train was brought to Fiesta Jumps to offer something truly unique in the local competition. We noticed that festivals, large birthday parties, and school events needed an attraction that could move around and entertain dozens of guests at once.

Unlike standard party rentals, our trackless train offers a real ride experience. It is beautifully crafted, safely driven by our professional engineers, and guaranteed to be the highlight of your event. From its authentic train bell to the colorful carriages, every detail is designed to wow your guests!

    Authentic Experience Real train bells, whistles, and beautiful craftsmanship.
